Usage: TypeWriter <Command> [Command Arguments]
 
Available Commands:
    TypeWriter Help                         (Show Help)
    TypeWriter Compile                      (Compile The 'src' Folder In Your Current Directory)
    TypeWriter Execute                      (Same As Execute But Also Runs It)
    TypeWriter Run <*.Dua> [Arguments]      (Run A Dua Archive)
    TypeWriter Setup                        (Create A src Folder In Your Current Folder)
